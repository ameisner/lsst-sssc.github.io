Getting Started

Welcome to the LSST Solar System Science Collaboration. This page is intended as a resource to get caught up with the basics.

Useful links:

  • A helpful glossary of LSST-related terms and acronyms - the page can be also used to suggest new terms to be addedd to the list
  • Crash Course on LSST and Rubin Observatory provided by Melissa Graham at the Vera C Rubin community forum. The page includes short (up to ~15 min) presentations on: The Planned LSST Data Products, Accessing and Analyzing LSST Data Products, Survey Strategy & Cadence Optimization, and Rubin Community Forum. It's an excellent place to start getting up to speed with the general LSST.
