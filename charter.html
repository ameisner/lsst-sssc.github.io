SSSC Charter

Motivation and Purpose of the LSST SSSC

The LSST (Large Synoptic Survey Telescope) Solar System Science Collaboration (SSSC) was created in 2006 with the main goal to facilitate the efforts of the planetary astronomy community to study the planets and small body populations within our Solar System using LSST data. The collaboration serves to coordinate the scientific community to develop methods and tools to analyze the data from LSST, as well as understand the optimum survey strategies for LSST to discover and study moving objects throughout the Solar System.
